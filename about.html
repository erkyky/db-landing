Leadership

Ying Huang

Co-founder

Before co-founding Deepblue, Ms. Huang launched C-Star SFR Advisors, where she successfully raised three residential funds, growing the firm's assets under management to $120 million.

Prior to C-Star, Ms. Huang served as a Director and Partner at Starwood Capital Group, one of the world's largest real estate private equity firms. Over her nine-year tenure at Starwood, she played a pivotal role in cultivating relationships with leading global investors, contributing to raising over $3.8 billion in equity. In 2016, she spearheaded Starwood's $4.5 billion hotel portfolio transaction, securing the largest equity commitment in Starwood's history.

Education: Harvard University (M.S. Real Estate) · Seoul National University (M.S. Urban Planning) · Fudan University (B.A.)

Anthony Liu

Co-founder

Before co-founding Deepblue, Mr. Liu served as a Managing Director in Acquisitions at Hines, a leading real estate investor and developer. He played a pivotal role in sourcing, acquiring, developing, and managing investments across multifamily, commercial office, and retail sectors within the New York metropolitan market.

Notable achievements include the development of One Vanderbilt and The Whit and the acquisitions of The Source at White Plains and The Hudson Square Portfolio. Prior to Hines, Mr. Liu was a member of the investments team at Jamestown Properties and an investment banking analyst at Wells Fargo.

Education: Carnegie Mellon University, Tepper School of Business (Finance) · CFA Charter Holder
